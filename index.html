Why Ingrid?

Why I Love Ingrid

First and foremost I adore her style. Her raw emotions bleed into the words of each song. Not only this but she carefully sets the mood with audio that accompanies the lyric.

She isnt afraid to be happy, sad, angry, or even a little crazy. She is possibly one of the best country artist around today. Check her out ya'll!
